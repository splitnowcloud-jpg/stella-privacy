Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Stella ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by FairPay LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Stella mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Stella:

  • Account information: email address, display name, and username when you register.
  • Messages: text messages, voice messages, images, and GIFs sent through the App to enable communication features.
  • Usage data: app activity such as messages sent, features used, and session duration for improving the App.
  • Device information: device type, operating system, and language settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Stella messaging service.
  • To enable real-time AI-powered translation of messages using Anthropic's Claude API.
  • To power the Stella AI assistant features within the App.
  • To send push notifications for new messages.
  • To improve and maintain the App.
  • To respond to support requests.

3. AI Translation and Processing

Stella uses Anthropic's Claude AI to translate messages and power AI features. Message content is sent to Anthropic's API solely to process your translation and AI requests. Anthropic does not use API data to train its models. We do not store message content beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure. Messages are stored to enable conversation history within the App. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic: for AI translation and assistant features (message content only, not personal identifiers).
  • Supabase: for database and authentication infrastructure.
  • Expo / EAS: for app delivery and push notifications.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data and messages for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Stella is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
